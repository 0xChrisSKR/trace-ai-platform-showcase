# Future Work

The canonical roadmap is maintained in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md).

The immediate work is validation rather than feature expansion:

- Produce a clean candidate build and gate run.
- Promote to the RC environment through a verified release path.
- Complete authenticated product UAT.
- Capture fresh screenshots from the promoted candidate.
- Validate connection-dependent Apps under real account conditions.

After those gates, the product can continue toward general solution recommendation, automatic workspace/App configuration, and verified public promotion.
